Istanbul, Turkey

×

Istanbul, a city that straddles Europe and Asia across the Bosphorus Strait, is a mesmerizing blend of ancient and modern. Its Old City reflects the cultural influences of the many empires that once ruled here.

Highlights:

  • Visit the iconic Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque
  • Explore the Grand Bazaar, one of the world's largest covered markets
  • Cruise the Bosphorus for stunning city views
  • Experience authentic Turkish cuisine and culture
  • Discover the underground Basilica Cistern
Best Time to Visit: Spring (April-May) and Fall (September-October)
Ideal Duration: 5-7 days
Starting Price: PKR12,999 per person
Inquire About Istanbul

Bali, Indonesia

×

Bali is a tropical paradise known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs. The island is home to religious sites such as cliffside Uluwatu Temple and thousands of Hindu temples.

Highlights:

  • Relax on pristine beaches in Seminyak and Nusa Dua
  • Explore ancient temples like Uluwatu and Tanah Lot
  • Experience traditional Balinese dance and culture
  • Visit the iconic Tegallalang Rice Terraces
  • Enjoy world-class surfing, diving, and yoga retreats
Best Time to Visit: April to October (dry season)
Ideal Duration: 7-10 days
Starting Price: PKR18,999 per person
Inquire About Bali

Swiss Alps, Switzerland

×

The Swiss Alps offer some of Europe's most breathtaking mountain scenery. From world-class ski resorts in winter to hiking and mountaineering in summer, the region provides year-round adventure amidst pristine natural beauty.

Highlights:

  • Ski or snowboard in world-renowned resorts like Zermatt and St. Moritz
  • Hike the spectacular trails of Jungfrau region
  • Ride scenic trains like the Glacier Express
  • Visit charming alpine villages and enjoy Swiss hospitality
  • Experience summer paragliding and winter skiing
Best Time to Visit: December-March for skiing, June-September for hiking
Ideal Duration: 7-10 days
Starting Price: PKR24,999 per person
Inquire About Swiss Alps

Kyoto & Tokyo, Japan

×

Japan offers a perfect contrast between ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity. Experience the serene temples and gardens of Kyoto alongside the neon-lit streets and technological wonders of Tokyo.

Highlights:

  • Visit Kyoto's iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine and Kinkaku-ji Temple
  • Experience the cherry blossom season (late March to early April)
  • Explore Tokyo's vibrant neighborhoods like Shibuya and Shinjuku
  • Ride the bullet train between cities
  • Enjoy authentic sushi, ramen, and tea ceremonies
Best Time to Visit: Spring (cherry blossoms) and Fall (colorful foliage)
Ideal Duration: 10-14 days
Starting Price: PKR27,999 per person
Inquire About Japan

Santorini, Greece

×

Santorini is famous for its dramatic views, stunning sunsets, white-washed houses, and beautiful beaches. This volcanic island in the Cyclades group of the Greek islands is one of the most picturesque destinations in the world.

Highlights:

  • Witness breathtaking sunsets in Oia
  • Explore the unique black and red sand beaches
  • Visit ancient archaeological sites like Akrotiri
  • Enjoy sailing tours around the caldera
  • Taste exquisite Greek cuisine and local wines
Best Time to Visit: May to October
Ideal Duration: 5-7 days
Starting Price: PKR21,999 per person
Inquire About Greece

South Island, New Zealand

×

New Zealand's South Island is an adventure lover's paradise with its snow-capped mountains, glaciers, fjords, and lush rainforests. It's also home to many filming locations from "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy.

Highlights:

  • Cruise through spectacular Milford Sound
  • Explore the adventure capital Queenstown
  • Visit the Franz Josef and Fox Glaciers
  • Experience Middle-earth at Hobbiton (North Island)
  • Enjoy hiking, bungee jumping, skiing, and more
Best Time to Visit: December to February (summer)
Ideal Duration: 10-14 days
Starting Price: PKR32,999 per person

Hunza Valley, Pakistan

×

Hunza Valley is a stunning mountain valley located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, ancient Buddhist monasteries, and rich cultural heritage, Hunza offers a unique blend of natural beauty and traditional charm.

Highlights:

  • Explore the ancient Buddhist monasteries of Hunza
  • Experience the stunning Hunza Valley landscapes
  • Visit the historic Baltit Fort and Baltit Palace
  • Enjoy trekking through the Karakoram mountain range
  • Discover the rich cultural heritage of the Hunza people
Best Time to Visit: March to May and September to November
Ideal Duration: 10-14 days
Starting Price: PKR32,999 per person
